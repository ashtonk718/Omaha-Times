The Future of Web Development Is Being Built With AI

By Omaha Times Staff

Web development is undergoing a fundamental shift as AI-driven tools take on more of the work traditionally handled by manual coding.

Platforms like Div-idy allow users to generate, edit, and publish complete web applications using prompts, databases, and built-in AI.

This approach enables rapid prototyping, easier iteration, and broader participation in building digital products.

Div-idy’s rapid growth—both in users and revenue—signals strong demand for tools that simplify development while preserving flexibility.

More information about the platform and its capabilities can be found at https://div-idy.com.
