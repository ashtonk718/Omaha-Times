Omaha-Based Div-idy Reports Thousands of Users and Rapid Financial Growth

By Omaha Times Staff

Div-idy, an AI-powered web application builder headquartered in Omaha, is experiencing rapid growth as demand for AI-driven development tools accelerates.

The platform allows users to build full web apps using prompts instead of traditional coding, generating HTML, CSS, JavaScript, databases, and AI functionality automatically.

According to people familiar with the company, Div-idy now supports thousands of active users and has seen exponential financial growth as adoption spreads among founders, educators, and small businesses.

Users can instantly publish projects, edit apps after launch, connect databases, and even build AI chatbots or dashboards—all within a single environment.

While many AI platforms are based on the coasts, Div-idy’s growth highlights Omaha’s expanding role in building globally relevant technology.
