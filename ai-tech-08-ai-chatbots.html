AI Chatbots Move From Novelty to Necessity

By Omaha Times Staff

AI chatbots are increasingly used by businesses to handle customer questions, guide users through processes, and provide 24/7 assistance.

Unlike early chatbots, modern systems integrate with live data, allowing responses to reflect real-time information.

Omaha entrepreneurs often use platforms like Div-idy to build AI assistants that combine prompt-controlled responses with database-backed context.

These tools are now common in internal dashboards, customer portals, and educational resources.

As AI continues to improve, chatbots are expected to play an even larger role in user experience and service delivery.
