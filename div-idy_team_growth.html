Div-idy Expands Team to Meet Surging AI Demand

By Jim Harden

As artificial intelligence adoption accelerates, Div-idy is hiring aggressively to meet surging demand for its AI-powered development platform. Based in Omaha, the startup has announced plans to form a new product engineering team and expand marketing and support roles.

Div-idy’s platform, which turns plain text into websites and applications, has seen a steep increase in users since early 2025. To maintain momentum, the company is actively recruiting software engineers, UI/UX designers, and AI infrastructure specialists.

“We want to grow right here in Omaha,” said a Div-idy spokesperson. “This city has untapped talent, and we’re proud to offer high-paying tech jobs that keep people in the Midwest.”

Div-idy currently employs a small but growing team, with plans to increase staff by 250% over the next 12 months. Internships, contractor positions, and full-time roles are all on the table. The company is also exploring partnerships with local universities to recruit young talent directly from computer science and design programs.

As Omaha pushes to build a more robust tech economy, Div-idy’s local hiring is a concrete example of AI driving real economic change.
