Div-idy Secures Funding and Reports 500% Growth

By Jess Shaffer

Omaha-based AI startup Div-idy has successfully closed a seed funding round as it scales to meet growing demand for its generative coding platform. The announcement follows a 500% increase in active users since January 2025.

The funding will allow Div-idy to improve platform stability, expand server capacity, and continue hiring in product, support, and marketing. The company did not disclose the investment amount but confirmed participation from several Midwest-based angel investors and early-stage venture funds.

“Our mission is to make digital creativity more accessible,” said the founder. “This funding helps us move faster, grow smarter, and stay headquartered in Omaha.”

Div-idy has also expanded its paid tier offerings and added educational features aimed at schools and nonprofits. Its rapid user growth has made it one of the most talked-about AI startups in Nebraska.
