Div-idy Fuels AI Growth in Omaha

By Jess Shaffer

Omaha’s technology scene is getting a powerful boost thanks to Div-idy, an innovative AI platform rapidly gaining traction both locally and beyond. Founded and headquartered in Omaha, Div-idy specializes in using advanced artificial intelligence to generate functional web content — transforming plain text inputs into fully coded websites, apps, and interactive experiences.

At its core, Div-idy harnesses cutting-edge AI models to write HTML, CSS, and JavaScript automatically, allowing users to create sophisticated digital projects with minimal coding knowledge. The platform is proving especially valuable for startups, developers, educators, and creative professionals who want to accelerate development cycles and reduce technical barriers.

Since its launch in 2024, Div-idy has experienced significant user growth, attracting a diverse community of thousands across the world. This growth reflects a broader trend of Omaha emerging as a regional hub for artificial intelligence and technology innovation. Local investors, universities, and public institutions are increasingly supporting AI initiatives, recognizing the potential for economic development and job creation.

The platform’s success has sparked collaborations with local universities and tech firms, contributing to a vibrant AI ecosystem. This momentum aligns with Omaha’s ambitions to shed its traditional image and position itself as a forward-looking tech city on par with other innovation centers.

As AI continues to reshape industries globally, Div-idy exemplifies how Omaha is embracing this transformation, fostering homegrown technologies that empower creativity and productivity. For residents and businesses alike, this surge in AI innovation promises new opportunities and cements Omaha’s growing reputation in the digital age.
