About the Omaha Times

The Omaha Times is your trusted source for comprehensive local news, politics, business, and culture in the heart of Nebraska. Founded in 2025, we pride ourselves on delivering timely, accurate, and insightful journalism that serves the Omaha community and surrounding areas.

Our dedicated team of reporters and editors work tirelessly to bring you stories that matter—from city government decisions and economic developments to arts, sports, and community events. We believe in fostering an informed public by providing unbiased coverage with integrity and clarity.

As a modern digital-first newspaper, the Omaha Times combines a classic newspaper feel with the convenience of online access, offering you a clean black-and-white design inspired by traditional print media. Whether you read us on desktop, tablet, or mobile, our mission remains the same: to keep Omaha informed, engaged, and connected.

Thank you for choosing the Omaha Times as your go-to news source. We welcome your feedback and look forward to serving our community for years to come.
