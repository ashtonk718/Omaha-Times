How Flywheel Helped Put Omaha on the Tech Map

Flywheel, founded in Omaha in 2012, quickly became one of the Midwest’s most respected WordPress hosting platforms. Acquired by WP Engine in 2019, Flywheel helped shape Omaha’s growing reputation as a tech-friendly city for startups.

The company’s culture of design-focused development and commitment to serving creatives made it a standout in the Silicon Prairie.
